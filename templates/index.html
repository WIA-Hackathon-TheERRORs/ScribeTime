Scribe Time

An Innovative Solution
For Finding Scribes

We aim at connecting Visually Impaired Students, Students with chronic medical conditions, students with cerebral palsy and students with temporary injuries like a fracture, etc, with the Voluntary writers who can assist them in writings exams.

Anyone who is a resident of India and have a minimum qualification of Metric Pass, can volunteer and help students in need.
